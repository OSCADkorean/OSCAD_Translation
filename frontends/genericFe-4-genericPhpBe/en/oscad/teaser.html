OSCAd Specific Disclaimer

The OSLiC and the OSCAd are only dealing with prototypic use cases. If you feel, that your specific case can't be specified by the OSLIC or is irregularly covered by one of the cases (false positives), please contact your legal expert. If you feel that your specific case is irregularly not covered by our taxonomy (false negative), contact us, please!
